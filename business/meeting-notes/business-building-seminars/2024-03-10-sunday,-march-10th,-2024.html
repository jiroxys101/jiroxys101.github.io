    • Listening

      • 6 Characteristics of Poor Listening Skills
        1. Judgemental Listening → Jumping to conclusions about the person you are listening to
        2. Selecting Listening → Only listening to what you want to hear; only responding to the content that is of your particular interest
        3. Impatient Listening → Waiting for the other person to finish speaking before you
        4. Egocentric Listening → Turning the conversation to oneself
        5. Patronizing Listening → Pretending you are listening when you are not
        6. Stubborn Listening → Listening but already having made up your mind
