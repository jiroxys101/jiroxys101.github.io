  1. How many people have I met one-on-one | how many new friendships have I developed
  2. Write down your goals for the month & rate yourself on the 9 core steps
  3. Go 2 deep for every 1 wide
    1. This is not limited to your personal legs, drive depth IN the leg at a 2 to 1 ratio of depth and width
    2. Depth requires A|B-List
  4. Thoughts are things, but most thougts are fake - thoughts about the past or future are simply a perspective. The only thing that truly has any significance are the thoughts we HAVE about those things IN THE MOMENT
