My notes center around the concepts of delayed gratification, hard work, empathy, personal growth, and sacrifice. The quotes I've gathered emphasize the importance of turning excuses into reasons, finding purpose through overcoming personal limitations, and discovering oneself through serving others. Notable figures such as Shalu Mahraj, Murli Mahraj, and Mahatma Gandhi underscore these principles.

In the context of achieving success, especially in ventures like going diamond in Amway, I am reminded that the best investment is in oneself, and the journey involves hard work and discipline. From mentors like Naren and Priya Kumar, and Jitsu and Reema Alam, I've learned the significance of empathy, understanding, and continuous personal growth. Sacrifice is also a recurring theme, highlighted by Luna Le's insight that one must sacrifice for their goals, or risk losing them. Finally, the importance of addressing issues promptly to avoid them escalating into major problems is stressed, aligning with the notion that what isn't my business should remain out of my focus.
