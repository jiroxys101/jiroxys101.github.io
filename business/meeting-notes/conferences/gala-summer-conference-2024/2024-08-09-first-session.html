Verified Customer Sales

  3. Sell out of your inventory
Do it Quickly and KEEP Doing it

Rafi and Jessica Lebron

Products & People
  • Our business has two pillars: products and people
    • Without products, our business produces no compensation
    • Without people, our business can not move products
  • Everyone has a network that they have the opportunity to maximize for their own benefit, and the benefit of those in their network

Jinen & Punkti Chedha

Having a Growing List
  • Having a constantly & consistently growing list is what will allow you to build your business from a position of strength and abundance

Sanjeev & Shauna Sahay

The Six Cardinal Rules
  • The Cardinal Rules Ground Your Business
  • Launch people of Quality → at your level or above
The Three "Don'ts"
  1. Don't mess with anybody's Ego (do not embarrass anyone)
  2. Don't mess with anybody's Money
  3. Don't mess with anybody's spouse
The Three "Nevers"
  1. Never do anything for the First time without checking with your growing upline
  2. Never pass negative to a downline or a cross line
    1. Use a "Sandwich" approach - Positive-Negative-Positive
  3. Never Embarrass your Upline or Downline
"Success is not complimentary. Pray as if it depends on God. Work as if it depends on YOU"

– Sanjeev Sahay

Jeremy & Aminata Siniu

How to Do Meet & Greets
  • You need to take ownership from the beginning
What is the purpose of a Meet & Greet?
  • Primary Goal: Get to know one another
    • Most important part of the process
  • Learn to Convey your own excitement to the candidate; if you don't seem passionate about what you're doing, it will be near Impossible to try to get someone else to catch the excitement
  • There is no timeline when completing a Meet & Greet
  • Listen to their story → their story will have clues that will help you direct the flow of conversation during the Meet & Greet
Talks to Listen To
  • BWW2083 Basics of MG1
  • Process Mindset by Sugeet Ajmani
  • BWW2043 Meet & Greet Mindset

Sid Sabane

Showing the Plan
  • The Growth of your Business is proportional to how many new people you show the Business Plan to
  • Options for Showing the Plan
    • One-on-One → Builds Connection
      • Transferring Belief needs to be done in-person
      • EVERYONE needs encouragement
    • Home Meeting
    • Board Plan/Team Meeting → Builds Conviction
      • The Environment of a BWW Event pulls people in
  • If you're worried about messing up, do it more
  • The worst plan is the plan that you don't show
  • The only number you have to think about is Twenty Plans a Month

Mansoor & Rumpa Wahidi

Follow Up & Follow Through
  • At the Follow Up Stage, you are already 80% of the way there
  • Be sure to provide your candidate with talks and ensure that they have taken the time to listen to them
    • If someone is NOT ready to learn (i.e. did not listen to the talks), they are not ready for entrepreneurship
    • Ask them questions about what their takeaways were
Addressing Questions
  • Write down the questions that they have
"If we are able to answer most if not all of these questions today, what, if anything, would stop you from getting started today?"

– Mansoor Wahidi

Resources
  • BWW2899 - Follow Up & Follow Through - Rob Shannon
  • BWW2578 - Putting it All Together - Esmin & Kulsuma Feritovich
