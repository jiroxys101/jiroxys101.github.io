Performance vs Sustained Performance

  • What is Sustained performance? → Performance that can be duplicated and maintained
  • What leads to Sustained performance? → Self-Leadership

Ask yourself: "Are you allowing your perspective to change because the business got tougher for you?"

3 Things That You Are Doing That May Be Keeping You From Succeeding

  1. Overthinking → Anything you are thinking that gets in the way of your goal is overthinking
  2. Living in the Past → Preoccupation with how things happened before can lead to burnout
  3. Inability to Deal with Uncertainty → Nothing is guaranteed; You need to be able to handle the ups and downs, and ADAPT

3 Types of People

  1. Givers → People who prioritize giving to others, without the necessity of having received first
  2. Takers → People who prioritize receiving from others without giving anything in return
  3. Matchers → People who prioritize receiving something from others before they are willing to give anything in return

  3. Equipping
